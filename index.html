About

Built by someone
who lives it.

I'm Kaylee Sawatsky — a paramedic who has worked shifts that leave you physically and mentally drained. I know what it's like to come home exhausted and wonder how you're supposed to take care of yourself when your job demands everything you have.

After years of struggling to find a program that actually fit my life as a first responder, I built one. Beyond the Call isn't a generic fitness plan — it's designed around the realities of shift work, high-stress environments, and the unique physical demands of emergency services.

I'm also a Certified Personal Trainer and Nutrition Coach, and I've combined everything I know to create a program that meets you where you are — and takes you further than you thought possible.
